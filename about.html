About Matowo Hardware

Established in 2005, Matowo Hardware has been a trusted partner for construction professionals and DIY enthusiasts across Kenya. We pride ourselves on quality products, expert advice, and exceptional customer service.

Our History

Starting as a small family business in Nairobi, Matowo Hardware has grown into one of Kenya's leading hardware suppliers with multiple branches across the country. Our journey has been built on trust, quality, and community service.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality hardware products and expert solutions that empower our customers to build, renovate, and create with confidence. We aim to be the first choice for all construction and DIY needs.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction are the core values that guide everything we do. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, suppliers, and communities.
